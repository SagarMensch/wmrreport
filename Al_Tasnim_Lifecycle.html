Meaning & Definition of Each Step

A comprehensive breakdown of every operational phase executed by Al Tasnim and how they are electronically tracked in the central database.

Phase 1: SAP Target Generation & Administrative Approvals

Before Al Tasnim can deploy mechanical equipment into the desert, immense bureaucratic structures must be cleared. This phase ensures the project holds legitimate SAP authorization, necessary materials, and financial backing from PDO.

  • EarliestEndDate: Master target schedule generated via SAP_DRILLING_SEQUENCE.
  • location_po_recvd_date: Location Purchase Order from PDO. Financial authorization.
  • date_-_material_available_at_site: Confirmation that physical inventory exists onsite.
  • flaf_issue_date: Final regulatory clearance granting permission to work.

Phase 2: Location Preparation & Civil Works

Phase 2 marks the beginning of physical footprint modification. Civil construction crews arrive to flatten the desert plot, stabilize the ground, and build the physical "pad" that is capable of safely supporting a million-pound drilling rig.

  • const._actual_start_date: The real world start-date of all physical labor.
  • location_preparation_status_in_progress_completed: Confirms pad readiness.

Phase 3: Flowline Construction & The Critical Hydro-Test

Running concurrently with Location Prep, crews lay miles of "Flowline" pipeline to connect the future wellhead to a centralized gathering manifold. This phase holds the greatest risk to operations.

  • flowline_construction_progress: Measured week-over-week.
  • actual_date_pcp_esp_vendor_delivered_the_skid: Pumping machinery is staged.
  • Bottleneck Rule: const._complete_date_including_f_l_final_hydro_test_1_day_before_rig_on_date. The flowline's hydrostatic pressure test MUST pass precisely one day prior to Phase 4. Failure here blocks the massive Rig from deploying.

Phase 4: Mechanized Rig Operations & Drilling (Spudding)

The highest cost phase. The enormous Rig apparatus is hauled to the site and erected. The drilling bit impacts the earth (an event called "Spudding") and aggressively digs to the geological Target Depth (TD).

  • actual_rig_on_date: Exact date the Rig reaches location.
  • well_name_after_spud: The administrative naming convention shifts.
  • progress: Daily and weekly footage metrics tracked in WellMonitoringReport.
  • NPT Scenarios: If actual_rig_on_date misses the SAP target dates, the OS flags Non-Productive Time (NPT) causing dramatic drops in Cum Progress.

Phase 5: Rig Demobilization & Tie-Ins

Immediately upon hitting total depth, the expensive primary Rig is dismantled and shipped to the next scheduled well. In its place, specialized Hoist or RSR units deploy to install downhole completion equipment and physically connect the well piping.

  • actual_rig_off_date: Marks the official end of primary drilling.
  • actual_hoist_fbu_rsr_on_date: Specialized mechanical completion crew arrives.
  • date_of_tie_in_port_readiness: Connecting the flowlines to the actual wellbore.

Phase 6: Commissioning & Bureaucratic Handover

The steel is buried, and the machinery is ready. Al Tasnim systematically pressures up the well and verifies production yields. Following the mechanical success, a race against paperwork deadlines begins to secure financial settlement.

  • actual_comm._start_date: Starts the startup procedure stream.
  • actual_comm._finish_date_with_in_2_days_from_actual_engg._completion_date: Speed KPI indicating operational efficiency.
  • rlmu_submitted_to_ho-date_with_in_7_days_from_actual_comm.: Critical Administrative Rule: Paperwork must reach head-office in 7 days or it triggers an NPT delay against revenue.
  • over_all_progress_percentages: Finalizes to 100%, completing the well.
